Cheesy Potato Coma

Ingredients

  • 2.5lbs unpeeled, thin skin potatoes (or 2 small bags of baby potatoes)
  • 1lbs ground beef
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 1tbsp olive oil
  • 1tsp garlic, minced
  • 1/2tsp salt
  • 1/4tsp black pepper, ground
  • 1/4tsp paprika
  • 1/4tsp thyme
  • 1 can of condensed cheddar cheese soup (undiluted
  • 1/3cup parmesan cheese (grated)
  • 2tbsp fresh dill or parsley
  • 1/2cup old cheddar (shredded, packed)
  • Green onion to taste

Directions

  • Heat oven to 425F
  • Cut potatoes into 1‚Äù cubes (if using baby potatoes, cut into quarters) and place in medium or large mixing bowl. Add onions, olive oil, garlic, salt, black pepper, and thyme. Mix well.
  • Place potato mix into 9x13 baking dish (sprayed with cooking oil) and roast for 30 mins., stirring once halfway through cooking time.
  • In a large frying pan or electric frying pan over medium heat, cook beef. Once beef is cooked, add cheddar soup, paprika, dill/parsley, and stir. Reduce heat to low and simmer.
  • Remove potatoes from oven and let cool while reducing oven temp to 375F
  • Pour beef/cheese mix onto potatoes and gently mix. Sprinkle grated cheese and green onion onto potatoes
  • Place dish in oven and bake uncovered for 25 mins.
  • Remove from oven and let cool for 5 min. and enjoy.
